HEY,

There are many tags I could attribute to myself: designer, photographer, researcher, scientist, or engineer, call it what you want. And there are so many more I’d be eager to add, but you can call me a collector of sorts. I strive to consistently add new knowledge, skills, and tools to my repertoire.

Recently, I completed my Masters of Design in Interaction Design at CCA. Previously, I graduated from the UC Berkeley with a Bachelors of Science in Bioengineering, Minor in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences, and Certificate in Design Innovation.

Currently, I’m in a pursuit to bring my past to my present, and craft everlasting solutions that adapt to the future. Search for a space where these experiences meld together, and I’ll meet you there.

—JAMES
